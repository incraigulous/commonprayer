# Compline

Compline is the final office of the day, prayed before sleep. It is brief, quiet, and meditative.

## Structure

1. Opening — "The Lord Almighty grant us a peaceful night and a perfect end."
2. Confession
3. Psalms 4, 31:1–6, 91, or 134
4. Scripture lesson (short)
5. Response — *Into your hands, O Lord, I commend my spirit*
6. Hymn — *Before the ending of the day*
7. Prayers — Lord's Prayer, suffrages, collect
8. Blessing and Dismissal — *Guide us waking, O Lord, and guard us sleeping…*

Compline is accessible from the **More** tab. It does not have Rite I / Rite II variants.
