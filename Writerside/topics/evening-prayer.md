# Evening Prayer

Evening Prayer (Evensong) mirrors the structure of Morning Prayer and is traditionally sung in cathedral and choral settings. The app renders the spoken form.

## Structure

1. Opening Sentences (seasonal)
2. Confession of Sin
3. Invitatory — *O Gracious Light* (*Phos hilaron*)
4. Psalter — psalms for the evening according to the 30-day cycle
5. First Lesson (Old Testament)
6. Canticle — *Magnificat* (Song of Mary)
7. Second Lesson (New Testament)
8. Canticle — *Nunc dimittis* (Song of Simeon)
9. Apostles' Creed
10. Prayers — Lord's Prayer, suffrages, collect of the day
11. Prayers of the People
12. Dismissal

## Rite I vs. Rite II

Rite I uses the traditional form throughout ("Lighten our darkness, we beseech thee, O Lord…"). Rite II uses contemporary language. Switch with the inline version tabs or change your default in [Settings](settings.md).
