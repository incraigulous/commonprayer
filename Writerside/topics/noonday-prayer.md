# Noonday Prayer

Noonday Prayer is a brief office for midday, taking about five minutes. It consists of:

1. Opening verse
2. One or two short psalms (Psalms 119:105–112, 121, 124, 126, or 128)
3. A short Scripture lesson
4. A brief response
5. The Lord's Prayer
6. Collect
7. Dismissal

The Noonday office does not have Rite I / Rite II variants — it is the same in all versions of the app.
