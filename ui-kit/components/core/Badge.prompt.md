A small-caps pill for liturgical metadata: the proper, season, or a marker.

```jsx
<Badge>Proper 9</Badge>
<Badge variant="rubric">The First Lesson</Badge>
<Badge variant="sage">Ordinary Time</Badge>
<Badge variant="violet">Lent</Badge>
<Badge variant="gilt">Feast Day</Badge>
```

- **variant**: `default` (ruled), `rubric` (emphasis), `gilt` (feasts), `sage` (green/ordinary), `violet` (Advent/Lent), `solid` (filled rubric).
