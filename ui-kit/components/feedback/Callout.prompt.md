A callout aside for content set apart from the flowing office — a note, a
collect, the closing blessing, or an antiphon.

```jsx
<Callout variant="prayer" title="The Collect">
  O God, the author of peace and lover of concord…
</Callout>

<Callout variant="blessing" title="Blessing">
  Gracious and compassionate God, slow to anger and rich in mercy…
</Callout>

<Callout variant="refrain" title="Refrain">
  Morning, noon, and night I cry out in my distress.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="note" title="A note on the day">…</Callout>
```

- **variant**: `note` (ruled), `prayer` (gilt frame), `blessing` (centred gilt wash, italic), `refrain` (rubric left-rule). **title** is optional; **glyph** overrides the leading mark.
