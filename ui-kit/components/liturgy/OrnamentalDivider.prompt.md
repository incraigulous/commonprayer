An ornamental divider for the quiet breaks between offices and readings.

```jsx
<OrnamentalDivider />                       {/* gilt maltese cross */}
<OrnamentalDivider glyph="fleuron" />
<OrnamentalDivider glyph="latin" tone="rubric" />
<OrnamentalDivider glyph="none" />          {/* plain hairline */}
```

- **glyph**: `cross` · `latin` · `fleuron` · `diamond` · `asterism` · `none` (or a custom string). **tone**: `gilt` (default) or `rubric`.
