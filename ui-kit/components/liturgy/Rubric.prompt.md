A rubric — the red-letter instruction that directs the worshipper.

```jsx
<Rubric>The Officiant begins the service with one or more sentences of Scripture.</Rubric>
<Rubric center variant="sm">All standing</Rubric>
<Rubric variant="heading">The Invitatory</Rubric>
```

- Italic rubric-red by default. **variant**: `sm` (smaller), `heading` (small-caps section mark). **center** to center. Renders `<p>` unless `as` overrides.
