A scripture reading set apart from the surrounding prose.

```jsx
<Scripture cite="John 4:6–32 (NLT)">
  <p>Jacob's well was there; and Jesus, tired from the long walk, sat
  wearily beside the well about noontime…</p>
</Scripture>

<Scripture cite="Psalm 95:1" variant="illuminated">
  Come, let us sing unto the Lord.
</Scripture>
```

- **cite** renders a small-caps citation; **mark** shows a gilt cross (default on).
- **variant**: `quiet` (rubric left-rule) or `illuminated` (full gilt frame) for the principal lesson.
