A versicle-and-response — the antiphonal call and answer of the office.

```jsx
<Versicle lines={[
  { by: 'Officiant', text: 'Lord, open our lips.' },
  { by: 'People', text: 'And our mouth shall proclaim your praise.', response: true },
]} />
```

- Each line has **by** (speaker mark, rubric-red small-caps — a word or a ℣/℟ glyph) and **text**. Set **response** to bold a congregational answer.
